Virtual Reality Club


    Ready to be immersed?

  • The Virtual Reality Club objective is to educate students about this exciting unique medium, as well as involve students in the process of creating VR content, problem solving, and other potential areas in which VR can bring positive changes.

  • We are here to offer a creative and fun community, a place where we can feel comfortable in socializing with each other, brainstorm ideas, as well as collaborating together to create VR experiences! It's all about teamwork.

    • Meetings will be every Tuesday, from 1pm to 2pm in ITEC 34, with our Faculty Advisor, Ms. Dallal!

    We hope you join us on this journey! Thank you!
